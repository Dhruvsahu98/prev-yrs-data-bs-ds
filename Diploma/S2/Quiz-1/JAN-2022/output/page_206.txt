Suppose that the number of customers visiting a KFC on a particular day is $N \sim Poisson(5)$. Assume that each customer purchases a Classic Chicken Zingers with probability 0.3, independent of other customers and independent of the value of $N$. Let $X$ denote the number of customers who purchase a Classic Chicken Zingers in a day. Let $Y$ denote the number of customers who do not purchase a Classic Chicken Zingers.
